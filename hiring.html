Hiring

I am recruiting post-doctoral fellows to work on large-data analytics in the economics of digitization. Two profiles are of particular interest:

Position 1 — Economics / Econometrics

  • PhD in economics, econometrics, statistics, or a related field
  • Background in microeconomics, game theory, and consumer-behavior theory
  • Experience with structural modeling and empirical research
  • Interest in applied Information Systems work

Position 2 — Computer Science / ML

  • PhD in computer science, statistics, or a related field
  • Expertise in machine learning and data mining (SVMs, gradient boosting, decision trees, etc.)

Both positions: proficiency in SQL and R; knowledge of Python (NumPy, SciPy, scikit-learn) is highly valued.

To apply, send a copy of your vita and a brief statement of research interests to miguel.godinhomatos@ucp.pt.
